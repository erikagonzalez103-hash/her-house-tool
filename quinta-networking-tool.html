Good to know

A few honest answers.

Where does my data live?

On your phone — nowhere else. Every connection you add is saved right here in this device's browser. It never goes to Quinta & Co., to a server, or to anyone. Actually yours means the list is yours, privately.

What if I clear my browser history?

Clearing your browsing data — or switching browsers, or getting a new phone — will erase the list, because it only lives here. There's no cloud copy to restore from.

So before you clear anything, export your contacts to Excel (the Export button, top right). That's your backup, and your way to move everyone into your real address book or CRM later. A good habit: export to yourself at the end of the event.

How do I add this to my home screen?

iPhone (Safari):

  1. Tap the Share icon in the address bar (the square with an arrow).
  2. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  3. Tap Add. It'll sit on your home screen like an app.

Android (Chrome):

  1. Tap the ⋮ menu (top right).
  2. Tap Add to Home screen (or Install app).
  3. Tap Add.

Do I need a Claude account to use it?

No. Nothing to sign into, nothing to install from an app store. It just works.
